Project overview

Technical Details

The work is purely JS / Canvas. No external libraries.

Influence

PbDi is influenced heavily by film narrative and the feeling of being in a theater (or drive in theater). Each piece includes generative prose and a screen like visual in an attempt to recreate the movie going experience, the feeling when the lights go down and a key scene plays out. Ideas that drove this:
  • Generative poetry (Especially slotted works, ie Alison Knowles / etc.)
  • Micro fiction / plot synopsis / detective writing / action tropes
  • Strange movie plots (Predator, Big Lebowski, Turner and Hooch)
  • Retro video game text dialog
  • Grids, microchips, and pegboards
  • Letterpress offsets / Plotter layering
  • Bauhaus design

Artistic approach

The artistic approcah is based on a variable grid of pegs (the pegboard) and different colors / outputs / crop are generated on top of behind it. Coming from a relief / screen printing background layers and opacity are very important to the visuals of the piece. Though it is a 2d work layering and opacity give depth.

Traits:

  • Background is white, black, or dark grey
  • Grid size can vary in 10 different sizes. A single peg grid is an outlier at 1/1000.
  • Color schemes generated are 3,5,9, and 10 palletes. Each produces a unique combination.
  • There are 30 unique color schemes. Colors were inspired by a number of sources and very curated. Picked off of from things like an old t-shirt print, a sundown, a dixie cup, art, etc...
  • There are 13 unique types of outputs *final list TBD* (weave, rings, blocks, puzzle, burst, gas, multiply, abacus, lines, bubble, diamond, pilled, webbed)
  • Cropped (controls if there are areas where our output will not display, the pegs are untouched)
  • Behind: true or false. Do the outputs happen on top of or behind the pegs
  • Hyper: Any output on top lightens the peg underneath it
  • SquarePeg: true or false. Is the peg round or square
  • Noisy: true or false. Is noise texture applied.

Prose:

The prose is very variable and dynamic but basically consists of 5 main prompts filled in with a combination of around 2100 unique words. I think there are around 100k combos possibe. Prose can sound absurdist (like many movies from the 80s) but should be structurally sound and make sense as a sentance. They are intentionally built to represent a movie plot or scene and allude to a larger story.
